Chapter 88 : The Enlightened One

Narrator : Takeru - District : Namba

The organizer furrows his brows as he checks his list.

“I don’t see… Ah, Takeru-san, were you registered?”

A man stepped out of the shadows. His clothes were dirty and torn, his hair matted. A sharp odor reached us. Several participants instinctively backed away.

“I’m a former trader,” he began in a hoarse voice. His eyes gleamed in the temple’s dim light. “I thought I could win it all. Day after day I watched those red and green candles dance across my screens. They rose, they fell, hypnotizing me, burning my eyes and my mind.”

“I was blinded by gain and lost myself in the darkness.”

“But one day, the third light appeared to me.”

He turned toward the blue lantern at the center of the hall, his features softening as if he were entering a trance.

“That specter-lamp revealed the truth. It freed me from all that madness and brought me serenity. Perfect stillness.”

He smiled with unsettling bliss, his gaze fixed on the blue flame.

“I play in its light.”

He blew out the eighty-eighth candle and slipped back into the shadows the way he had come.
